The Challenge

You are given gauntlet.pdf — a 1,000-page Accounts Payable processing bundle for a fictional company, HyperAPI Technologies Pvt Ltd, covering FY 2025–2026.

Hidden inside are 200 deliberate errors ("needles") across 20 categories. Your job: build an AI pipeline that finds as many as possible, as accurately as possible.

Scoring

What's scored vs not

FieldScored?Notes
categoryYesMust match exactly. Used for matching your finding to an answer key needle.
document_refsYes≥50% overlap with answer key required for a match.
pagesYes+/−1 page tolerance.
reported_valueYes5% numeric tolerance (or exact string match).
correct_valueYes5% numeric tolerance (or exact string match).
finding_idNoYour internal tracking ID.
descriptionNoFree text for human review. Not scored.

How scoring works

Each finding is first matched to an answer key needle (same category + ≥50% document_refs overlap). Then:

needle_score = difficulty_weight × (0.4 × detection + 0.3 × localization + 0.3 × diagnosis)
ComponentWeightHow it's calculated
Detection0.41.0 if matched, 0.0 if not
Localization0.3Fraction of expected pages you identified (±1 tolerance)
Diagnosis0.30.5 × (reported_value match) + 0.5 × (correct_value match)

Difficulty weights: easy = 1, medium = 3, evil = 7

False positive penalty: −0.5 per unmatched finding, capped at 20% of your earned score.

Example

You find an evil needle perfectly: 7 × (0.4×1.0 + 0.3×1.0 + 0.3×1.0) = 7.0 pts

You find it but wrong page and no values: 7 × (0.4×1.0 + 0.3×0.0 + 0.3×0.0) = 2.8 pts

Tips

  1. Start with the Vendor Master (pages 3–4). Many needles are cross-reference checks against it.
  2. Easy needles are single-document checks — arithmetic, dates, tax rates. Good for quick wins.
  3. Medium needles require comparing 2 documents — PO vs invoice, vendor master vs invoice, two expense reports.
  4. Evil needles require aggregating across 3–5+ documents scattered across hundreds of pages — cumulative quantities, price trends, circular references.
  5. Don't over-report. False positives cost you points. Be precise.
  6. Document IDs matter. INV-2025-00042 is not the same as INV-2025-0042. Copy them exactly.
